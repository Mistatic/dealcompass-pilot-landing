ABOUT DEALCOMPASS

Who we are, what we do, and how we pick

We built DealCompass to save people time. Instead of digging through endless listings, you get a ranked shortlist focused on value and confidence.

What DealCompass does

We scan a large number of product opportunities and narrow them down to a short list worth your attention.

  • We prioritize strong value over hype.
  • We rank picks so you can compare options quickly.
  • We keep each category list simple and easy to scan.

What “high-signal picks” means

High-signal picks are products that stand out for clear, practical reasons:

  • Good value: the price-to-value looks meaningfully better than typical options.
  • Confidence signals: we have enough evidence to trust the pick belongs in the shortlist.
  • Category strength: it compares well against other options in the same category.

How to read our confidence labels
