## 2) High-priority content/page ideas (5)

2. **Best Home Office Deals by Category**
   - Evergreen hub page segmented by keyboards, mice, hubs, lighting, and accessories.
3. **DealCompass Methodology**
   - Explain ranking signals, buy/no-buy criteria, and update cadence for trust + E-E-A-T.
4. **Top Ergonomic Upgrades Under $100**

5. **Remote Work Setup Starter Kit**
   - Bundled recommendations by budget tier with internal links to active picks.

## 3) Internal linking recommendations

- Keep the sitewide link path: Home (`/`) -> Past Picks (`/past-picks.html`) and Past Picks -> Home.
- Add contextual text links from homepage sections to Past Picks using natural anchors:
  - "See historical picks"
  - "View our past deal archive"
- When additional content pages launch, link from each new page to:
  - current picks on homepage
  - past picks archive
  - methodology page (once published)
- Use descriptive anchors with target keywords (avoid generic "click here").
- Maintain a lightweight footer navigation block across all pages for crawl consistency.
